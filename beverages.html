BEVERAGES

Virgin Mojito

Mojitos are like those cool drinks you get with fresh lime juice, mint leaves, and a bit of sweetener, all mixed with soda or sparkling water and some rum. They're super refreshing and perfect for hot days or when you just want something light and tasty.

Blue Lagoon

A Blue Lagoon is a vibrant and refreshing cocktail typically made with vodka, blue curacao liqueur, lemonade, and citrus soda. It's a beachy, summery drink that is a popular, festive choice for summer bashes, big and small. The mocktail version omits the vodka and uses blue cura√ßao syrup instead of the liqueur. It still features lemonade and citrus soda and is garnished with a cherry and lemon slice. It's a stunning, tasty beverage!

Hurricane Mocktail

This Hurricane Mocktail is a naturally sweet blend of passion, pineapple and citrus juices, with raspberry-lime sparkling water as a pink upper layer. Garnished with maraschino cherries, this recipe is free of red dyes and doesn't need simple syrup. Enjoy for Mardi Gras or any festive occasion!
